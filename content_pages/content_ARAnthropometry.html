AR Anthropometry

An AR application aiding interior and furniture design


| Individual Project by Di Ba, 2020 | 5 min reading time

Overview


The current AR application projects virtual human models onto the physical world, aiming to help interior designers, furniture designers and design students understand human scales and their relationship with the surrounding environment. The human models were made regarding Henry Dreyfuss Associates’ study ‘The Measure of Man And Woman’ (1993). The study significantly influenced the development of ergonomics in design disciplines.

Henry Dreyfuss Associates ‘The Measure of Man And Woman’ (1993)



Anthropometry Models


Three groups of human models were made according to different age/sex: the adult male, adult female and children. According to the statistics, different body sizes and gesture models were made in each group.
