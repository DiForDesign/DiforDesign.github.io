About Me


My speciality is sustainable design, while I am also a well-trained product designer and graphic designer. I have made a brochure that has effectively helped our client company communicate with fellow corporations and gain reputations in the industry.

I am passionate about connecting people with their natural inner selves through design. I am often fascinated by how much internal power we possess.

Having been in multicultural environments (China and the UK), I have developed my knowledge, empathy, communication and critical thinking skills.

Through professional studies, I obtained a wide range of creative skills, including ethnographic research, graphics, video production, rapid prototyping, interactive programme, VR and AR applications etc. These skills organically contribute to my design works.
